About ForgeFinds

What We Do

ForgeFinds is an independent tech deals discovery platform. We automatically curate and surface the best deals on gadgets, gaming products, home-theater equipment, and tech hardware from trusted retailers like Amazon.

Our goal is simple: help tech enthusiasts find great deals without the noise. Every deal is vetted for current pricing and availability.

How It Works

We use automated systems to monitor retailers and aggregate tech deals across multiple categories. Our deal database is updated daily with the latest discounts, price drops, and limited-time offers. Each deal includes real-time pricing information and direct links to retailers.

Editorial Independence

ForgeFinds is editorially independent. We are not owned by, endorsed by, or affiliated with any retailer (beyond affiliate partnerships). Our deal selection is based purely on value and relevance to our audience — we don't promote deals because retailers pay us to.

We prioritize transparency: all deals include current pricing, discount percentages, and when applicable, coupon or promotion details.

How We Make Money

We earn affiliate commissions when you click through our links and make a purchase. This costs you nothing extra — you pay the same price whether you click our link or go directly to the retailer. We disclose this in our Affiliate Disclosure.

Our Standards

  • Accuracy: We verify prices and availability before publishing deals.
  • Relevance: We focus on gadgets, gaming, and tech products of genuine interest to enthusiasts.
  • Transparency: We disclose all affiliate relationships and how we earn revenue.
  • No Spam: We don't list low-quality products or "deals" that aren't actually valuable.
