Affiliate Disclosure

ForgeFinds is a participant in the Amazon Associates program and other affiliate networks. When you click a deal link on our site and make a purchase, we may earn an affiliate commission at no additional cost to you.

What This Means

When you see a deal on ForgeFinds that links to Amazon, Best Buy, or other retailers, that link is an affiliate link. If you click it and complete a purchase within the retailer's tracking window (typically 24-90 days), we receive a small commission.

Important: You Pay the Same Price

Our affiliate status does not affect your price. You will pay the exact same amount whether you:

  • Click our link and purchase
  • Go directly to the retailer and purchase
  • Use the retailer's app or physical store

The commission comes from the retailer's marketing budget, not from inflating product prices.

How We Earn Money

ForgeFinds is funded entirely through affiliate commissions. This allows us to:

  • Maintain and improve the deals database
  • Run automated deal-finding systems
  • Keep the site free and ad-free for users

Affiliate Programs

We are currently affiliated with:

  • Amazon Associates: Affiliate links to Amazon products
  • Other Retailers: Best Buy, Newegg, and other tech retailers (as applicable)

No Editorial Bias

Our deal selection is not influenced by affiliate commission rates. We feature the best deals based on:

  • Genuine discount value (not fake MSRP markups)
  • Product quality and relevance to tech enthusiasts
  • Accuracy of pricing and availability

A $5 off deal on a great product will be featured before a 1% commission higher-margin product we don't believe in.

FTC Compliance

This disclosure complies with the Federal Trade Commission's Endorsements & Testimonials Guides. All affiliate relationships are disclosed to ensure transparency with our audience.
