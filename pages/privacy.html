Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

ForgeFinds ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site.

Information We Collect

Browsing Data: When you visit ForgeFinds, we may collect analytics data through Google Analytics, including your IP address (anonymized), pages visited, and device type. This helps us understand how users interact with the site.

No Personal Data: We do not require you to create accounts, log in, or provide personal information to browse deals.

Cookies: We use minimal cookies for analytics and affiliate tracking. You can disable these in your browser settings.

How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Track affiliate link conversions (required by our affiliate partners)
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to track anonymous usage patterns. Google's privacy policy applies to this data: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Affiliate Networks: Amazon Associates and other affiliate programs may track your clicks to ensure we receive proper commission attribution. Review their privacy policies for details.

External Links: When you click a deal link, you leave ForgeFinds and are subject to the retailer's privacy policy.

Do Not Sell My Info

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Period.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics' default settings. Affiliate tracking data is controlled by Amazon Associates and other affiliate partners.

Security

ForgeFinds uses HTTPS encryption to protect data in transit. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We recommend standard security practices on your end.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be noted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of ForgeFinds constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
